Services

Pro Bono was founded in 2020 as part of the TCP Program. What started as a loose confederation of neighborhood-based legal services programs has grown into the nation's largest civil legal services provider. Our plan is to help more than 110,000 low-income Georgians annually. Pro Bono provides users with substantial value by incorporating an active forum one stop shop legal resources and a collaborative system to effectively pair student attorneys with people in the community in need of legal services.
